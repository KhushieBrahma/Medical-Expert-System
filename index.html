How This Expert System Works

This page uses a simple rule-based inference engine in JavaScript. Based on your selected symptoms, it matches predefined rules and displays:

  • A rough guess of the possible condition type
  • A general medicine category (not specific drug names)
  • Basic advice and a reminder to consult a real doctor
